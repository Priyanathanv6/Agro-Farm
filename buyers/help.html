Help & FAQ

What is Agricultural Decision Support System (ADSS)?
ADSS is a platform designed to connect farmers, buyers, and agriculturists to improve agricultural productivity and profitability through real-time information and market support.
How do I register as a buyer?
You can register as a buyer by filling out the buyer's details form on the buyer page with your contact and delivery information.
How can I communicate with farmers?
Use the real-time chat feature on the chat page to communicate directly with farmers for inquiries, negotiations, and support.
What payment methods are accepted?
Currently, payment options include online payment gateways such as credit/debit cards, UPI, and net banking integrated in the checkout page.
How can I track my orders?
You can track your orders from the orders page by logging in and checking the order status.
Who can I contact for technical support?
For technical support, please contact our support team via the help page or email support@adss.com.
